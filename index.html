DIY Recipes for Wellness and Beauty

Unleash your creativity with DIY recipes incorporating Valor oil, from homemade skincare products to wellness elixirs. Here are some creative DIY recipes incorporating Valor oil:

1. Valor-infused Body Butter:

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup shea butter
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil
  • 10-15 drops Valor essential oil
  • 5 drops Lavender essential oil (optional for added relaxation)
  • 5 drops Frankincense essential oil (for skin support)

Instructions:

  1. In a double boiler, melt the shea butter and coconut oil until fully liquefied.
  2. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.
  3. Add the Valor and Lavender essential oils (and Frankincense if desired) to the mixture and stir well.
  4. Place the mixture in the refrigerator for about an hour or until it begins to harden.
  5. Whip the mixture with a hand mixer until it becomes light and fluffy.
  6. Transfer the body butter to a clean container and store in a cool, dry place.

2. Valor Relaxation Bath Salts:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Epsom salts
  • 1/4 cup sea salt
  • 10-15 drops Valor essential oil
  • 5 drops Chamomile essential oil (for added relaxation)
  • Dried lavender buds (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the Epsom salts and sea salt.
  2. Add the Valor and Chamomile essential oils to the salts and mix thoroughly.
  3. If desired, add a handful of dried lavender buds for extra aroma and relaxation.
  4. Store the bath salts in an airtight container.
  5. To use, add a few tablespoons of the mixture to your bathwater and enjoy a soothing and aromatic bath.

Can you put valor directly on skin?

Valor is an essential oil blend created by Young Living, often used for its calming and uplifting properties. However, it is important to note that undiluted essential oils should not be applied directly to the skin, including Valor, as they can cause skin irritation or allergic reactions.

It is recommended to dilute essential oils with a carrier oil, such as coconut or jojoba oil, before applying them to the skin. Always perform a patch test and follow proper dilution guidelines to ensure safe usage.

What can I mix with valor essential oil?

Valor essential oil can be mixed with a carrier oil, such as jojoba oil, coconut oil, or almond oil, to create a diluted blend for topical application. Typically, you would mix a few drops of Valor with a carrier oil of your choice to dilute it before applying it to your skin. This can be used for massages, applying to the wrists or neck, or for a relaxing foot massage.

Additionally, you can mix Valor with other essential oils, like lavender or frankincense, to create a personalized aroma and therapeutic effect based on your preferences and needs. Always ensure you follow proper dilution guidelines and perform a patch test when blending essential oils.
